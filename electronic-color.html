The computer in the service of artists, engineers, and scientists is emerging as the key visual communication tool for our information age. Titles listed under Electronic Color bring together the art and science of color as they relate to computer graphics and to graphic computing. Topics covered range from the physics of light and color, application guidelines for effective use of color in computer graphics, to options for converting the on-screen image to paper. The highly recommended books are well illustrated, clearly written, and reasonably accessible to the motivated reader.
